~~~ recipe-info
Title: Pancakes
~~~

### Ingredients

~~~ recipe-ingredients
2 C all-purpose flour
3 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 3/4 C milk
1/4 C vegetable oil
2 large eggs
~~~


### Directions

Add all ingredients to a large mixing bowl and whisk until smooth.

Pour 1/4 cup batter onto a medium-hot griddle. Flip when the cakes start to bubble.
