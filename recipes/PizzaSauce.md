~~~ recipe-info
Title: Pizza Sauce
~~~

### Ingredients

~~~ recipe-ingredients
1 14.5 oz crushed tomatoes

1 6 oz can tomato paste

4 cloves garlic, minced

2 tsp dried parley

1 tsp dried oregano

1 tsp dried basil

2 tbsp olive oil

1 tsp salt
~~~

### Directions

Combine all ingredients in a small bowl.

Spread the finished sauce liberally on the pizza crust.
